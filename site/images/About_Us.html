Our Mission

Animal shelters do their best to take in lost, abandoned, abused, neglected, and sick animals but doing so as well as holding on to those animals until they’re adopted takes a hefty price. Due to the “No-Kill Movement”, euthanasia rates in shelters have dropped by around 85% in the past 25 years. This has saved the lives of millions of adoptable animals, but it comes at a price. The essence of the No-Kill Movement is that all healthy or treatable animals brought into a shelter will be fixed up and remain in the shelter until they’re adopted. But treating, vaccinating, and taking care of these animals costs a lot of money, and the result is that No-Kill shelters can only take in a limited amount of animals before closing their doors to new ones. This is why 20% of all the purchases of Pure Pawz puppy pads will go directly to The Humane Society of the United States for more animals to find a good home.

However, we are not only working to save our pets, but the planet as well. Our pads are made in factories powered by 80% renewable energy, and use a third of the waste of traditional pads of the same size, all while using channel lock technology to absorb liquids three times faster than our leading competitors, and dry out in under five minutes without a drop getting onto your floor.
